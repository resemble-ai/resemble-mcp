Voice Design lets you generate bespoke AI voices from text prompts. Describe the persona you need—accent, tone, energy, style—and Resemble returns candidates you can promote to full voices.

## Why Use Voice Design?

- Craft voices that match your creative direction or brand
- Rapidly prototype characters for games, podcasts, or assistants
- Iterate without recording new datasets

> Features and voice quality improve as we incorporate feedback, so keep experimenting and share results with the team.
